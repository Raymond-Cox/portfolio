Who I am

I grew up in an extremely impoverished county in southern Ohio. After graduating high school in 2010, I went to college to pursue a business degree. Just after completing my freshman year, I became a father to a darling girl. I dropped out of college shortly after to work full-time at my past summer job in residential construction.

The hours were daylight to dark. The pay was terrible. The work - backbreaking. Despite the terrible conditions, I did what I had to do to provide for my family. After nearly a decade, I decided that I couldn't stay in this career long-term. I spent spare time devising a plan of attack to escape construction during the next winter layoff.

I began my self-taught coding journey learning through online resources and courses in December 2017 (Shout out to freeCodeCamp!). I started my professional career 9 months later in September 2018 as a mobile developer. I'm now a senior software engineer who loves the startup environment.

I've lived a few different places since becoming a developer, but I currently reside in the beautiful small town Hastings, Michigan. I recently purchased a camper, so my plans for 2023 are to take many road trips around the country while working the digital nomad life.

Making the career change to developer was a defining time of my life that has opened more doors and opportunities than I could have ever imagined. One thing is for certain -- that I am an unstoppable human.
